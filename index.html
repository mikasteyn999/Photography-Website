ABOUT ME


Hi there!

I'm Mika & I'm a photographer living in the beautiful coastal town of Still Bay, South Africa.

My shooting style is candid, honest, natural & detail orientated. I use mainly natural light & the setting of your choice, where ever you feel most comfortable.

I'm an easygoing, spontaneous, social & open minded person who loves the outdoors & getting to know new people.

I look forward to capturing your special moments, be they large or small. I'm willing to travel anywhere in South Africa or beyond :)
